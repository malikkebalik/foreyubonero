What is Imani Showalter's Net Worth?

Imani Showalter is an American realty TV star who has a net worth of $2 million. Imani Showalter first achieved a modicum of recognition performing with a New York-based musical group. She is the mother of three children with athlete Stephen Jackson, a shooting guard and forward for the Milwaukee Bucks. The two were engaged, but ultimately did not get married. She joined the cast of the VH1 reality series "Basketball Wives: LA", during its first season, which began airing in August of 2011.

The "Basketball Wives" series is a spin-off of VH1's popular docu-drama "Baseball Wives". The series follows the lives of wives, girlfriends, and exes of famous basketball players. The first season of "Basketball Wives: LA", was notable for the number of fights between cast members, and has been renewed for a second season, though no airdate has been announced. So far, only one cast member has confirmed that they will be returning for the second season.

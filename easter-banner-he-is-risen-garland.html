Easter Banner He Is Risen Garland

Easter Banner (“He Is Risen” Garland)

Enjoy this free printable Easter banner. This garland says, “He Is Risen,” and the background is the text from Luke 24 about the resurrection of Jesus!

You can click on the images below for individual printables or here (“He Is Risen” Printables) for the package of all printables!

Each letter is about 3 1/2″ wide. If that is too large for your mantel, the “print preview” function of your browser may allow you to scale the size. I chose to scale it to 85%. Or, it the PDF opens in Adobe, on the print options, you can select “tile” and then scale by %.

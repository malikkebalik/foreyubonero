The Young and the Restless Spoilers: Wednesday, May 8 Nates Hidden Audra Agenda Lily Gets

The Young and the Restless (Y&R) spoilers for Wednesday, May 8, reveal that Nate Hastings (Sean Dominic) will have a drink with Audra Charles (Zuleyka Silver) as they catch up at the jazz lounge. It’ll lead to Nate getting honest with Audra and perhaps warning her about the terrible road she’s headed down with Tucker McCall (Trevor St. John).

Audra won’t seem concerned and may argue that she understands Tucker on a different level than anyone else.

After Nate presumably tries to steer Audra away from a future with Tucker, she’ll suspect Nate has a hidden agenda.

It looks like Audra will flirt with Nate a bit and wonder if maybe he’s trying to keep the door open for their romance instead.

There’s definitely an attraction between Nate and Audra, so that could grow and bring temptation for these two eventually.

In the meantime, Tucker will make a move that stuns Ashley Abbott (Eileen Davidson) – though it may be “Ms. Abbott” who really gets the shock since she’s in control right now.

By the end of this interaction, “Ms. Abbott” may become even more convinced that the only solution is to get rid of Tucker permanently!

Tucker could land in risky territory if he’s not careful, so we’ll pass along more predictions on what “Ms. Abbott” might do to him.

Other Y&R spoilers say Lily Winters (Christel Khalil) will get ambushed at the office, where Mamie Johnson (Veronica Redd) will arrange a surprise meeting.

Although Lily will quiz Mamie about the reason for it, Mamie will want to wait until everyone else arrives before she explains.

Lily will seem worried about what Mamie is up to and will eventually be forced to make a difficult decision once this meeting takes place.

Mamie will have some big news, but Lily may not be on board with whatever she’s got cooking up this time!

It’s clear that Mamie thinks she knows best, but will Lily put her foot down or cave in after this announcement?

The Young and the Restless spoilers say Lily will reveal her choice after hearing Mamie’s latest pitch, so stay tuned for updates on what this will mean for Chancellor-Winters.

What is Josep Maria Bartomeu's Net Worth?

Josep Maria Bartomeu is a Spanish entrepreneur and the president of FC Barcelona who has a net worth of $100 million. Josep Maria Bartomeu was born in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain in February 1963. He took over as president of the soccer team FC Barcelona in 2014 when Sandro Rosell resigned. Bartomeu is the partner and CEO of the companies ADELTE and EFS.

During his time as president of the team he has won the La Liga, the Copa del Rey, the UEFA Champions League, the UEFA Super Cup, and the FIFA Club World Cup. Bartomeu served on the board of FC Barcelona while Joan Laporta was president. When Rosell took over, Bartomeu was the vice-president, and became elected as the 40th president of Barcelona in 2014.

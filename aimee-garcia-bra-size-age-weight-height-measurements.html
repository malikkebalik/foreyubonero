Aimee Garcia Bra Size, Age, Weight, Height, Measurements

Biography:

Aimee Garcia was born on November 28, 1978 in Chicago, Illinois, U.S. Her first film appearance was as Jodi in The Homecoming, while her television debut was as Leanne Lawler in ER.

She has since appeared in other television shows including Resurrection Blvd., Angel, American Family, MDs, Las Vegas, CSI: NY, Lovespring International, CSI: Crime Scene Investigation and many others.

She became known for her television roles as Veronica Palmero in George Lopez, Marisa Benez in Trauma and Jamie Batista in Dexter. Her film work includes D.E.B.S., Spanglish, A Lot Like Love, and Universal Signs, among others.

Kajols comments about Pathaan collections spark a huge controversy

Kajol’s comments about Pathaan collections spark a huge controversy

Published on Jul 16, 2023 5:03 pm IST

Bollywood ever-green pair Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol share a very good rapport. The duo delivered some of the biggest blockbusters in Indian cinema, including everyone’s favorite, Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge. The actress is busy promoting her web series, The Trial, which is now streaming on Disney Plus Hotstar.

In one of the interviews, Kajol commented about Pathaan collections, which have now sparked a huge controversy on social media. The interviewer asked what she would ask Shah Rukh Khan. After giggling, Kajol said she would ask Shah Rukh, “How much did Pathaan really make?”

These words by Kajol triggered heated debates on social media. A few are commenting that Pathaan’s box office collections are fake. A few Shah Rukh fans are not happy with the comments of Kajol and started to troll her. The video clip of Kajol has been doing rounds on social media since morning.

As per the makers, the spy action thriller collected a whopping gross of 1050 crores worldwide. We should wait and see if Kajol would give any clarity regarding her statement.

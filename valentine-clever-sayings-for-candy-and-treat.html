Valentine Clever Sayings for Candy and Treat

Valentine Sayings for Kids

Here are some fun sayings for kids to attach to candy or treats for friends (at school, in the neighborhood, at church, etc.) on Valentine’s Day!

Please let me know if you have more ideas to add to this list!

Almond Joy:

It’s a joy being your friend!

Your friendship fills me with joy!

Blow Pop:

You blow me away!

Your friendship blows my away!

Brownies:

I’m shooting for brownie points!

Your friendship is better than brownie points!

Button Candy:

You’re as cute as a button!

Candy (Any Kind):

It’s sweet to have you as a friend!

Thank you for being a sweet friend!

You’re as sweet as they come!

Candy Bar:

Bar none, you are a great friend!

Cake Pops:

My heart pops for you!

To a “pop”ular kid!

Wanted to pop the question: Will you be mine?

You’re a “pop”ular friend!

You’re the icing on the cake!

You take the cake!

Chocolate Gold Coins: 

Don’t ever change!

Your friendship is like gold!

Chocolate Strawberries:

I like you “berry” much!

Life would be un”berry”able without you!

You are “berry”special!

You are a “berry” good friend!

Cookies:

You are one smart cookie!

Ding Dong:

Don’t be a ding dong, be my friend/Valentine!

I would be a ding dong without you as a friend!

Only a ding dong wouldn’t want you as a friend!

You’re no ding dong; you’re a great friend!

Donut holes:

I like you a “hole” bunch!

Donut you know I think you’re a great friend!

Dum Dum Suckers:

Don’t be a dum dum, be my friend/Valentine!

I would be a dum dum without you as a friend!

Only a dum dum wouldn’t want you as a friend!

You’re no dum dum; you’re a great friend!

Fortune Cookies:

I am so “fortune”ate to have you as a friend!

Fruit Snacks:

Don’t be fruity, be my friend/Valentine.

It’s fun to be fruity with you!

Fun Dip:

It’s fun to have you as a friend!

Thank you for being a fun friend!

You’re so fun!

You put the fun in my life!

Goldfish:

Glad we’re in the same school!

Will you o”fish”ally be mine?

You’re a great catch!

You’re the only fish in the sea for me!

Gum or Gum Tape:

I “chews” you as my friend!

Let’s stick together!

You blow me away!

Gummy Bears:

I like you “beary” much!

Life would be un”bear”able without you!

You are “beary”special!

You are a “beary” good friend!

Gummy Worms:

I’m hooked on you!

I dig you!

Hot Chocolate Pack:

You melt my heart!

You warm my heart!

Lifesavers:

Your friendship is a lifesaver!

Lollipop:

My heart pops for you!”

To a “pop”ular kid!

Wanted to pop the question: Will you be mine?

You’re a “pop”ular friend!

Milk Duds:

Don’t be a dud, be my friend/Valentine!

I am a dud without you as a friend!

Only a dud wouldn’t want you as a friend!

You’re no dud; you’re a great friend!

Mints or Mint Patties:

We’re “mint” to be friends!

Muddy Buddies:  

Thanks for being my buddy!

You’re a great buddy!

Nerds:

Don’t be a nerd, be my friend/Valentine!

I am a nerd without you as a friend!

Only a nerd wouldn’t want you as a friend!

You’re no nerd; you’re a great friend!

Nuts:

Don’t be a nut, be my friend/Valentine!

I’m a nut without you as a friend!

I’m nuts about you!

Only a nut wouldn’t want you as a friend!

You’re no nut; you’re a great friend!

Peeps:

Of all the peeps in the world, you are a great friend!

You are one of my favorite peeps!

Popcorn:

My heart pops for you!

To a “pop”ular kid!

Wanted to pop the question: Will you be mine?

You’re a “pop”ular friend!

Pop Rocks:

My heart pops for you!

You rock!

To a “pop”ular kid!

Wanted to pop the question: Will you be mine?

You’re a “pop”ular friend!

Reese’s Pieces:

I love you to pieces!

Rice Krispie Treats:

It’s a treat being your friend!

Ring Pops:

My heart pops for you!

To a “pop”ular kid!

Wanted to pop the question: Will you be mine?

You’re a “pop”ular friend!

Rock Candy:

You rock!

Rolos:

I like the way you roll!

Smarties:

Happy Valentine’s day smartie pants!

I wouldn’t be so smart without you as a friend!

It’s smart to have you as a friend.

You are one smartie!

Soda:

I “soda” know you are a great friend!

I “soda” think you are amazing!

Starbursts:

I am bursting with joy that you are my friend!

You’re a star!

Family Guy Predicts Caitlyn Jenner Transition

June 3, 2015 2:14 PM EDT

A Family Guy clip is going viral as some are saying it eerily predicted Caitlyn Jenner’s gender transition. Jenner, formerly known as Bruce, unveiled her new name and look in the July edition of Vanity Fair.

Since then, a clip from a 2009 episode of the show has resurfaced, thanks to YouTube user qro4yzeyl0i. In the clip below, talking baby Stewie Griffin is discussing Jenner’s son Brody, with Brian, the talking family dog. In response to a question about Brody’s birth, the pet says “Bruce Jenner is a man,” to which Stewie replies, “No, Brian, that’s what the press would have you believe but he’s not. Bruce Jenner is a woman. An elegant, beautiful, Dutch woman.”

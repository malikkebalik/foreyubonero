Dorothy Lucey Net Worth | Celebrity Net Worth

Dorothy Lucey net worth and salary: Dorothy Lucey is an American entertainment reporter who has a net worth of $3 million dollars. Dorothy Lucey is a graduate of George Washington University, where she majored in Political Science. Rather than going into a career in politics, she shifted her focus to television reporting. She began her television career working for WNEP in Pennsylvania as an on-air reporter. From there, she relocated to New York City to work for WABC.

This led to a job with NBC Sports, and a co-hosting position on the Lifetime talk show, "Attitudes". She was hired to work on "Good Day L.A.", a Fox Channel morning show in Los Angeles, in the mid-90s. She served as the show's entertainment reporter for the next seventeen years, but her contract was not renewed after 2012. She covered reality shows such as "American Idol", and often reported on celebrity gossip and scandals.

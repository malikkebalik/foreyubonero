What is Jacob Roloff's Net Worth?

Jacob Roloff is an American television personality who has a net worth of $700 thousand. Jacob Roloff was born in Oregon in January 1997. Jacob is featured in the TLC reality TV series Little People, Big World. The show follows Jacob's family and focuses on his parents Matt and Amy and his brother Zach who are all diagnosed with dwarfism. Unlike his brother and parents Jacob is of normal height. He lives with his family at Roloff Farms near Portland, Oregon.

Jacob has been featured in nearly 200 episodes of Little People, Big World since 2006. In 2012 he was featured in the spin-off series Little People, Big World: Wedding Farm. He has also appeared twice on The Oprah Winfrey Show. Jacob and family friend Mike Detjen were injured in 2006 on the family farm when a pumpkin catapult prematurely launched. He underwent surgery due to a small piece of his skull pushing into his brain. The accident was not recorded on film.

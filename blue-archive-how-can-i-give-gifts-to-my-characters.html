Blue Archive - How can I give gifts to my characters?

AppGamer Answered:

You can also give gifts to students while hanging out in the Cafe.

1. Tap the Gifts button in the lower-left corner to see all available gifts in your inventory.

2. From there, you can just tap and hold and try to drag it out. As you do this, smiley icons will appear above the heads of the students in the cafe.

3. Drop/release the item on the character you want to give the gift to.

These icons will determine the amount of relationship points that will be boosted by the gift. A normal smiley icon will just give a normal amount of RP while a smiley with a wide grin signifies a larger amount of RP to be boosted on the recipient.

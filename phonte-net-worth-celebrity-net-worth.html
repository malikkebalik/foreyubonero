What is Phonte's Net Worth?

Phonte is an American rapper who has a net worth of $1 million. Phonte was born in Greensboro, North Carolina in December 1978. He is half of the duo The Foreign Exchange and was a member of the trio Little Brother. Phonte's solo debut studio album Charity Starts at Home was released in 2011 and reached #9 on the US R&B chart and #61 on the Billboard 200 chart. He also released the album No News Is Good News in 2018.

The Foreign Exchange formed in 2002 and released their debut studio album Connected in 2004. They also released the album Leave It All Behind in 2008. Their album Authenticity was released in 2010 and reached #23 on the US R&B chart. The Foreign Exchange released the album Love in Flying Colors in 2013 which reached #22 on the US R&B chart and their album Tales from the Land of Milk and Honey was released in 2015 and reached #12 on the US R&B chart.

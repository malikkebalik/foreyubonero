Keanu Reeves Net Worth, Early Life, Personal Life, Career, and Others

Keanu Reeves is a Canadian actor, producer, and musician who made his feature film debut with 1986’s “Youngblood”. He has starred in many movies among which a lot have achieved immense success.

What Is the Net Worth of Keanu Reeves?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, the estimated net worth of Keanu Reeves is around $380 million he gained massive popularity for starring in the film “The Matrix” and the John Wick franchises.

He made merely $3000 from his 1986’s film debut, “Youngblood”. His performance in Bill & Ted’s Excellent Adventure earned him $95,000 whereas the 1994 film Speed earned him $1.2 million. He declined $11 million to star in the sequel to Speed saying he was not available as he was touring with his band.

His role in Johnny Mnemonic earned him $2 million whereas “The Devil’s Advocate” earned him $8 million. As of this writing, his total earnings from “The Matrix” tops $200 million from all sources. All this has contributed a lot to building Keanu Reeves’ net worth. 

After taking a dig into his net worth, let’s have a look at Keanu Reeves’ early life.

Early Life

Keanu Reeves was born on September 2, 1964, in Beirut, Lebanon. He is the child of Patricia and Samuel. His parents met for the first time when his mother was working as a costume designer in Beirut. Though the majority of his childhood was spent globe-trotting with his mother following his father leaving the family when Keanu was only three years old, they ultimately settled in Toronto, Canada in 1971.

His education suffered as he struggled with dyslexia. He dropped out of many high schools. But he set off for his lackluster grades on the ice, succeeding as a goaltender for his high school hockey team. He acted in a school production of Romeo and Juliet and realized his passion to perform, Keanu left hockey and school in pursuit of an acting career.

Personal Life

Throughout the 90s, Keanu Reeves was in a relationship with Jennifer Syme, and on December 24, 1999, they had a stillborn daughter, Ava Archer Syme-Reeves. The misery impacted their relationship eventually leading to their break up after many weeks. Syme met with a car accident in April 2001 and later died.

At the time, Keanu was set to start filming The Matrix sequels back-to-back and wanted time to cope with the tragic event prior to when he started shooting. Reeves was in a relationship with China Chow in 2008. He has never been married and is extremely private with regard to his romantic life.

Keanu isn’t religious; however, he is interested in Buddhism and calls himself spiritual.

In 2014, two stalkers broke into his home in the Hollywood hills. On September 12, the first happened when he woke up to a stalker in his library who told him she was there to meet him. After calmly talking to her, he called the police.

After 3 days, a second stalker entered his home through a gate that actually his cleaners left unlocked. The stalker took off the clothes and took a shower in his bathroom prior to swimming naked in his pool. During that time, he wasn’t at home but the cleaning crew informed the police and the stalker was removed.

He also founded a cancer charity but decided to not attach his name to it. He is a supporter of PETA, Stands up to Cancer, and the SickKids Foundation.

Career

Keanu Reeves’ career has earned him immense fame and net worth. He began his acting career with small roles on Canadian television, commercials, and several stage productions, in 1986, he appeared on the big screen for the first time with “Youngblood”.

Later, Reeves co-starred in several projects that including a major role in the 1986 successful film “River’s Edge”. The 1989 Bill and Ted’s Excellent Adventure became a hit at the box office, which earned more than $40 million domestically. He then starred in many films, both major and independent, along with the drama Point Break and the indie cult-hit My Own Private Idaho.

Keanu Reeves was shot into A-list status with the 1994 film Speed, which won two Academy Awards and grossed around $350.5 million worldwide. He then starred in The Matrix franchise, a trilogy of sci-fi action movies that brought him further success, grossing over $1.6 billion dollars. His role as Neo received a lot of love from audiences and the film gained massive popularity.

Keanu Reeves then starred in the 2014 action thriller “John Wick” where he portrayed the role of a retired hitman. The film was critically and commercially successful. He then reprised his role in two later films. His John Wick franchise has earned over $500 million at the box office.

His movies have earned over $4.6 billion at the box office across the world after adjusting for inflation as of this writing. This project also played a major role in Keanu Reeves’ net worth.

Glasgow, Scotland organized “KeanuCon”, Europe’s first Keanu Reeves film festival in 2019. It was held on April 27th and 28th and featured nine of his films over the two days.

Other Ventures

Apart from their acting career, Keanu Reeves’ other ventures also helped him in building his net worth. During the 90s, he played bass guitar for the band Dogstar for several years. In addition, he is an avid motorcyclist and co-founded a company that makes personalized motorcycles, Arch Motorcycle Company.

He is also the co-founder of a production company named Company Films. Keanu founded a small press named X Artists’ Books. He has even authored two books named “Ode to Happiness” and “Shadows”.

Emma Roberts spent her holiday weekend soaking up the sun.

The actress, who recently announced she's expecting her first child, shared a selfie via her Instagram story on Monday, writing "Happy Labor Day" across the image. In the photo, she's seen wearing a black, strapless swimsuit from J.Crew covered in red flowers. The garment is currently sold out at the time of writing.

She completed the poolside look with red, Mulberry sunglasses, which are currently available on Farfetch for $286.

Earlier in the week, Roberts showed off her chic maternity style with a mirror selfie, this time wearing a white minidress with bell sleeves. The garment, made from crushed cotton, was designed by Maurie & Eve and retails for $88.

Who is Lele Pons and when Did She Get Married?

Lele Pons, whose real name is Eleonora Pons Maronese and who was born on June 25, 1996, is a YouTube personality, actor, and singer of Venezuelan and American descent.

Pons rose to notoriety on Vine, where she was the platform’s most-followed woman and the third most-followed Viner with 11.5 million followers, before Vine’s shutdown in 2016. As of the end of 2022, she had amassed over 17 million fans on YouTube thanks to her comedy sketches.

She has now published her own music, co-written a novel, and appeared in numerous films, TV shows, and music videos. Currently, she is the star of the YouTube Original docuseries The Secret Life of Lele Pons and the host of the Best Kept Secrets with Lele Pons podcast on Spotify.

When and where was Lele Pons Born?

Doctor Ana Maronese Pivetta and Architect Luis Guillermo Pons Mendoza had their daughter Eleonora Pons Maronese on June 25, 1996, and she was born in Caracas that same day. Her father came out as gay when she was young, and that led to his separation from her mother.

She is the niece of Chayanne, the famous Puerto Rican singer, and is of Italian and Venezuelan ancestry herself. She was just five when she and her family emigrated to Miami, Florida, in the United States. It was in 2015 that she completed her education at Miami Country Day School and then relocated to the Los Angeles area.

She has revealed that she “got wounded to make them laugh” because she had a hard time establishing friends in high school. She has spoken about being tormented in high school, saying things like, “I’m not like the cool girls—I’m the other girl. The one who freely admits to being a nerd.

Is Lele Pons Married?

Pons moved to Miami with her family when she was five years old, but she did not become a United States citizen until she was 23. Pons started dating Guaynaa, a Puerto Rican rapper, in December 2020; the couple became engaged in August 2022 and married four years later, on March 4, 2023.

What Is Zack '24' Carpinello's Net Worth?

Zack Clayton Carpinello is best known as “24” amongst Jersey Shore: Family Vacation fans. He is engaged to Jersey Shore OG Jenni “JWoww” Farley, but not many fans know much about Carpinello’s personal life. Find out how much the reality star’s fiancé is worth. 

Zack Clayton is a professional wrestler and reality TV star 

Carpinello wrestles under the name Zack Clayton. The Bethlehem, New York native has always aspired to wrestle for the Worldwide Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). He graduated in 2013 from Bethlehem Central High School and began his journey training to become a pro wrestler.

According to an interview with 411 Mania, Carpinello always admired Ric Flair. “You couldn’t look at him on TV and not just stop what you’re doing and just watch,” he told the outlet. Today, Carpinello has six years of experience, according to CageMatch.net. He has been wrestling for All Elite Wrestling as Zack Clayton since 2015.

Outside of wrestling, Carpinello is also a fitness guru who owns the Zack Clayton Shop. The online store sells shirts and hoodies for men, women, and kids. Fans can also sign up for Carpinello’s newsletter to receive fitness and motivation tips in their inbox.

He is also on the rise to becoming a reality star. Carpinello has appeared on Jersey Shore: Family Vacation since 2019. Now, considering his newly-engaged relationship status, fans can expect to see more of Carpinello in future episodes of Jersey Shore: Family Vacation. 

Zack ’24’ Carpinello and Jenni ‘JWoww’ Farley are engaged 

Carpinello proposed to the reality star on Feb. 27, 2021, her 35th birthday. “On 2.27 I said yes on the top of the Empire State Building,” Farley captioned a set of photos on Instagram. In the photos, Farley and Carpinello kiss with champagne and look out over the New York City skyline. “You’re my forever,” Carpinello commented on Farley’s post. Later, he shared the news with his social media followers. 

“My always and forever,” Carpinello captioned the same set of photos. “You made me the happiest I’ve ever been. I love you more than anything.” Jersey Shore: Family Vacation stars, including Angelina Pivarnick’s husband, Chris Larangeira, congratulated Carpinello. “Congrats my guy,” he wrote. 

Zack Clayton Carpinello’s Net Worth in 2021

The wrestling world keeps a tight lid on their contracts, so it’s hard to say the exact details of how much a pro wrestler earns. Wrestlers don’t work under a union. Instead, each performer negotiates a contract and salary. Essentially, this means each wrestler’s net worth differs wildly from anyone else’s. According to Forbes, the main source of income for WWE wrestlers is their base salary. One of the lowest base salaries Forbes mentioned was $52,000 a year, while the highest was $5 million a year. The outlet also said on average, WWE wrestlers earn $500,000 annually. 

Based solely on his wrestling career, an estimate of Carpinello’s net worth is $500,000. Given his work on reality TV and his fitness ventures outside of wrestling, Carpinello is likely worth more than $500,000 in 2021. Despite his net worth possibly being more than half a million dollars, Carpinello’s fiancé is worth more in 2021. Farley has a net worth of $4 million.

What is Steve Parrish's Net Worth?

Steve Parrish is an English television personality and former motorcycle and truck racer who has a net worth of $10 million. Steve Paris was born in Cambridge, England in February 1953. He became a professional racer in 1976 at the age of 22 and won the ACU Solo title in the British Motor Cycle Championship. He was the 500cc ACU Gold Star Champion in 1987 and won the Shell 500 title in 1979 and 1980, as well as a Superbike title in 1981. Steve retired from motorcycle racing in 1986 and started truck racing as well as managing a Yamaha factory team to three British Superbike championships. He won the 1987 British Open Truck Racing Championship and won the European and British Truck Racing championships in 1990.

He retired in 2002 as the most successful truck racer of all time. Parrish works for ITV as a commentator and analyst for their coverage of Isle of Man TT. He previously commentated for BBC Radio and television. He has been called upon as an expert witness for motor racing incidents and holds a Guinness Book of Records world record for Fasted Speed Achieved in Reverse at 85 miles per hour. He co-authored Barry: The Story of Motorcycling Legend Barry Sheene in 2008. Steve has two children with his ex-wife Ruth.
